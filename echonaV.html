About EchoNav

EchoNav is a mobile application designed to provide audio-based navigation for visually impaired users in indoor environments. Our goal is to enhance accessibility and reduce dependence on others, allowing users to confidently navigate familiar spaces like homes, offices, schools, and universities.

Features

  • Voice Commands: Map environments, key landmarks, and define routes using simple voice commands.
  • Step-by-Step Voice Guidance: Clear, step-by-step voice instructions for navigation.
  • Obstacle Detection: Real-time detection and alerting of obstacles.
  • Offline Functionality: Works offline to ensure reliability without an internet connection.
  • Ease of Use: User-friendly interface with large buttons and clear instructions.
